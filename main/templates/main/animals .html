Wild Animals of Kenya 🐘

🦁 Lion

The king of the jungle, lions are apex predators known for their strength, courage, and social behavior. They live in prides and are found across Kenya's protected reserves.

🐘 Elephant

The largest land animals, elephants are highly intelligent and known for their memory. They play a vital role in shaping their habitats and are symbols of Kenya's wildlife.

🦓 Zebra

With their distinctive black and white stripes, zebras are herbivores that travel in large herds. Each zebra has a unique stripe pattern, like a fingerprint.

🦒 Giraffe

The tallest land animals, giraffes are iconic symbols of African wildlife. Their long necks allow them to reach vegetation other animals cannot, making them perfectly adapted to their environment.
